A Handy Laptop Stand

I made this Handy Laptop Stand as an end semester project of EDCAD in Semester-II, under the guidance of Prof. Ravi Butani and Prof. Nikhil Chotai at Marwadi University. The idea behind was to make a portable/handy thing which should be small in size and easy to carry with a laptop. The stand should have a minimum number of joints and it should be able to carry the weight of standard laptop size. It was completely made through Laser Cutter Machine.


Skills Used - Design Thinking Methodology, Fusion-360, Lasser Cutter
